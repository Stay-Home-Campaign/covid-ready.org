Campaigns built so far

We've built a bunch of campaigns targeted at specific audiences around the world who may not know enough about the coronavirus pandemic or what they can do to help their loved ones.


#StayHome

Informing people why they need to self-isolate for the good of themselves but also other people.

#BaliTaxiSafe

Advice to taxi drivers in Indonesia on how they can best protect themselves from spreading covid.

#StopTouristing

A message to tourists in Asia to stop travelling so much as they pose a threat to the others.

Start a campaign


  • 1) Find a specific community you want to target such as "backpackers in asia" or "people with cancer in the UK".
  • 2) Send us your proposal here and we'll fact-check it and respond within a couple hours. We're typically very quick!
  • 3) We'll get your campaign live on our website and you take full ownership over spreading it as far as possible through social media.
